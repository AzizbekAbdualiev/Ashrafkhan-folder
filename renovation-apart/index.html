What is a general contractor?

A general contractor — also known as a renovation contractor, remodeling contractor, or simply “GC” — is the main construction professional overseeing all aspects of a major remodeling project in NYC. From planning and permitting, to demolition, and through construction, your New York City contractor will work directly with you, their team of subcontractors, and all other construction professionals required for your project, which might include architects, designers, expediters, or engineers.

Your Sweeten GC will also work with your condo or co-op board to get necessary approvals for your remodeling project, which can be a common stumbling block in the early stages of a renovation. They will usher your remodeling project from concept to completion to deliver a quality result that is on time and on budget.
